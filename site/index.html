About

At CalcHvac Inc., we unite architecture, engineering, and energy modeling in one powerful platform. From Building Information Modeling (BIM) to real-time EnergyPlus simulations, we make it easy to design ASHRAE-compliant, energy-efficient buildings with data-driven precision. Our platform simplifies material optimization, integrates regional codes, and delivers ESG-ready reporting—helping teams measure sustainability, reduce energy waste, and make smarter design decisions through a clean, collaborative interface built for real-world projects.

Why US?

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision

At CalcHvac Inc., we bring architects and engineers together to design buildings that perform better and waste less energy. Our mission is simple — make energy modeling easy, accurate, and built right into the design process.

From ASHRAE checks to climate-based simulations, we take care of the details so teams can focus on good design. No extra software. No tech hassle. Just smarter decisions, better comfort, and real collaboration from start to finish.

Our vision at CalcHvac Inc. is to redefine how buildings are designed—making sustainable, high-performance design the standard, not the exception. We aim to empower every architect and engineer with intuitive tools that blend creativity with data, turning energy modeling, compliance, and ESG reporting into an effortless part of everyday design practice. By bridging technology and teamwork, we’re shaping a future where every building is efficient, compliant, and built with purpose.
